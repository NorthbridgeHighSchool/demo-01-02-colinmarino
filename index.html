Quarantine

As everyone knows, summer of 2020 was engulfed by a pandemic. Everyone had to quarantine in their homes for most the summer. Some people hated it, while others loved it. Me for example, I liked it. Being stuck in my own home was not as big of deal as a thought it would be. After the first few weeks of chaos, everything started to get better.

Because I knew we were going to be stuck at home with nothing to do, I took advantage of that. Everyday I got the most sleep I've ever had in my life. With nothing to do and nowhere to go, I had all the time that I could wish for to be abl to sleep. After needing to wake up at 6:00am everyday to go to school, I was extremely tired. All of this free time allowed me to catch up on all the sleep I'd missed, and get a little extra. Sleep is always something I look forward to in the summer, and this year I hit the jackpot.
